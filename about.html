About Me

IT Helpdesk Analyst & Healthcare Technology Specialist

I provide reliable, human-centered IT support for enterprise environments. Over 8+ years, I’ve supported large user bases across Windows, macOS, and Linux—owning onboarding/offboarding, executive support, and daily incident/request handling through ITSM platforms. My focus is keeping people productive and systems secure across healthcare and high-stakes operations.

I work across the Microsoft 365 ecosystem (Exchange Online, SharePoint, Teams, OneDrive), endpoint management with Intune and basic JAMF, identity with Active Directory, and the networking fundamentals that keep everything communicating (TCP/IP, DNS, DHCP, VPN, firewalls). I document thoroughly, automate where it helps (PowerShell, Bash, Python), and collaborate closely with Infrastructure and Security teams to deliver long-term, stable solutions—not quick temporary fixes.
