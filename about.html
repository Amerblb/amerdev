About Me

IT Helpdesk Analyst & Healthcare Technology Specialist

I provide reliable, human-centered IT support for enterprise environments. Over 8+ years, I’ve supported large user bases across Windows, macOS, and Linux—owning onboarding/offboarding, executive support, and day-to-day incident/request handling through ITSM platforms. My focus is keeping people productive and systems secure, especially in healthcare and high-stakes operations.

I work across the Microsoft 365 ecosystem (Exchange Online, SharePoint, Teams, OneDrive), device management with Intune and basic JAMF, identity with Active Directory, and the networking fundamentals that keep everything talking (TCP/IP, DNS, DHCP, VPN, firewall basics). I document thoroughly, automate where it helps (PowerShell, Bash, Python), and partner with Infra/Sec teams to drive durable fixes—not just quick patches.
